First, try closing the app completely and reopening it. If it keeps crashing:
  1. Go to your phone's Settings → Apps → Math Drill → Clear Cache
  2. Restart your phone
  3. If the issue persists, uninstall and reinstall the app from Google Play (your data may be lost)
  4. If it still crashes, email us with your phone model and Android version
Open Math Drill → go to the Stats tab → tap "Upgrade to Pro". On the upgrade screen, scroll down and tap "Restore Purchase". Make sure you are signed in to the same Google account you used when you originally purchased Pro. Restore is free and unlimited.

  1. Close the app fully and reopen it

If ads still appear after restoring, please email us with your order number from Google Play (found in your Google account → Payments).
Unfortunately, progress data is stored locally on your device and is not backed up to any cloud server. When you uninstall the app, this data is deleted. We are planning to add optional backup functionality in a future update. Your Pro purchase however can always be restored — it is tied to your Google account, not your device.
Google Play allows refund requests within 48 hours of purchase. To request a refund:
  1. Open the Google Play app on your phone
  2. Tap your profile icon → "Payments & subscriptions" → "Order history"
  3. Find the Math Drill Pro purchase and tap "Request a refund"
After 48 hours, refunds are at Google's discretion. For special circumstances, you can also email us.
